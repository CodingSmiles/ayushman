MAIN AVIONICS LOGICBOARD 1 - DATA RECORDING

Main Flight Avionics - MAL1

MAL1 or the Main Avionics Logicboard is the flight computer of AGM1 (Autonomously Guided Module) . MAL1 records constant flight data like, Altitude, Temprature and Atmospheric pressure data using aBMP280 and logs it into a SD card using a SD card adapter. MAL1 is one of the main components of the rocket as it records data, to know more about MAL1, go to the 'MAL1' page above or click on 'Learn More' below.
