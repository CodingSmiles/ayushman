MAIN AVIONICS LOGICBOARD 1 - DATA RECORDING

MAL1 or the Main Avionics Logicboard is the flight computer of AGM1 (Autonoumsly Guided Module). MAL1 records flight data like, Alitude, Temprature and Atmospheric Pressure using a BMP280 and logs it into a SD card using a SD card adapter. MAL1 is one of the main components of the rocket as it records data, to know more about MAL1, go to the 'MAL1' page above or click on 'Learn More' below.
